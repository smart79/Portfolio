About Me

Stephen brings over 6 years of Full Stack Web Development experience, 8 years in SEO and Web Design, and 10 years in Digital Marketing. After serving 5 years in the United States Marine Corps, he pursued his education, earning an Associate’s degree in Computer Information Science from Odessa College, graduating Summa Cum Laude. Committed to expanding his skill set, he continued his education by obtaining a Full-Stack Web Development Certification from Southern Methodist University (SMU) and offering website design services to the public.

Over the past 10 years, Stephen has built a successful career as a Digital Marketing Manager, working with two financial services startups and contributing to two Fortune 500 companies—Hilton and U.S. Money Reserve. He also managed SEO and digital marketing accounts for one of Austin’s largest search engine optimization firms, Yodle (acquired by Web.com). Stephen furthered his education by earning a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the University of Texas at Dallas, Erik Jonsson School of Engineering this past December. With expertise in web technologies, automation, and e-commerce, and a strong entrepreneurial drive, Stephen specializes in developing scalable web applications and optimizing digital experiences to create impactful solutions.
