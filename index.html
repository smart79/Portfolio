About Me

Chris brings over 3 years of SEO and Web Design services experience, 4 years of Front End Web Development experience and 6 years of overall Digital Marketing experience. After serving 5 years in the United States Marine Corps, he went on to study to earn his associate degree in Computer Science at Odessa College. Over the past 15 years Chris has enjoyed a successful career as Digital Marketing Manager with two successful Financial Service startups, Digital Marketing Manager for 2 fortune 500 companies, including the largest Search Engine Optimization Company in Austin.

After graduating from The Full-Stack Web Development program at Southern Methodist University in Dallas Texas, Chris' passions for Web Technologies and Internet Marketing combined with his entrepreneurial spirit and expert SEO and Marketing experience lead him to seek out a career in Web Development that would combine these strengths. As a UI/UX zealot, Chris continues to hone his coding skills everyday to improve his ability to create and develop.
