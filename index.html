About Me

Stephen brings over 5 years of SEO and Web Design services experience, 4 years of Full Stack Web Development experience and 6 years of overall Digital Marketing experience. After serving 5 years in the United States Marine Corps, he continued his education earning an Associate's degree in Computer Information Science at Odessa College and graduating Summa Cum Laude. Stephen decided to improve to his ability to create and develop by continuing his education through obtaining certifications and offering website design services to the public.

Over the past 15 years, Stephen has enjoyed a successful career as a Digital Marketing Manager with two successful Financial Services startups, worked for 2 fortune 500 companies in Hilton and US Money Reserve, and managed SEO and Digital marketing services accounts for one of Austin's largest Search Engine Optimization Companies Yodle, now Web.com. After graduating from the Full-Stack Web Development certification program at Southern Methodist University in Dallas Texas, Stephen's passions for web technologies and internet marketing combined with his programming experience and entrepreneurial spirit drives him to seek out a career in Web Development that would combine these strengths. Stephen is now in his senior year at the University of Texas at Dallas earning his Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and working as a freelance Web Developer and owner of SiteSensei.net in Rockwall, Tx.
